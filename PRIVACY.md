# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

At Power App, we believe in privacy as a fundamental right. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles information.

## Information Collection and Use

**Power App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

- **No Personal Information**: We do not ask for or collect any personal information such as your name, email address, or location.
- **Local Data Only**: All battery data, device names, and settings are processed locally on your Mac. No data leaves your machine.
- **No Analytics/Tracking**: We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking software.

## Device Permissions

To function correctly, the application requires certain permissions:

- **Bluetooth**: Used exclusively to discover and communicate with supported battery packs (e.g., Ecoflow, Bluetti, JK-BMS) via Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE).

## Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
